Investing made effortless for Gen Z

ChangeUp helps you grow wealth without changing your habits. We round up your spare change from everyday purchases and invest it automatically — all inside WhatsApp. No apps. No spreadsheets. Just smart, consistent investing built for your life.

How ChangeUp Works

Every time you make a purchase — like a €2.60 coffee — ChangeUp rounds it up to €3.00 and invests the spare €0.40 into a curated portfolio. Over time, these small amounts grow into meaningful investments, all without lifting a finger.

Investing that fits your lifestyle

No spreadsheets, no complicated apps — just friendly, chat-based investing that works right inside WhatsApp. ChangeUp adapts to your spending habits and turns everyday purchases into steady financial growth. Plus, you’ll earn streaks, get nudges, and see your progress — without pressure or jargon.
